01 About

I'm a Software Engineering undergraduate at SLIIT building full-stack web and mobile products. I care about clean architecture, thoughtful interfaces and shipping work that actually feels good to use.

Currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Information Technology specializing in Software Engineering, I've spent the last few years turning coursework and personal ideas into real, working applications — from AI-assisted health platforms to microservice-based telemedicine and civic-transparency apps.

I move comfortably across the stack: React and Tailwind on the front, Node, Express and Spring Boot on the back, MongoDB and SQL for data, plus Flutter and Android for mobile. I'm equally happy in Figma sketching a flow as I am debugging an API.
